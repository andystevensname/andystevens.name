This series of websites is a remarkable effort to capture a moment. Every volume is worth exploring; Vol. II: What Color Was the Sky rather poetically paints the picture you might not have seen last night as the sun set, and is my favorite. They just released Vol IV: taken, an alarming look at how much information you forfeit just by visiting a website, and how easy it is to track you based on that information. Great website authorship, through and through.
