When I read A Heartbreaking Work of Staggering Genius' I was gobsmacked by Eggers' use of footnotes, drawing, and other literary accoutrement--it felt like he used every feature of the printed book form to tell a story. HTML Review delights that same senses for online works. Even if you don't like the words, what the works here accomplish is, well, staggering.
